About Us

We’re building the most engaging, accessible typing trainer—fast, accurate, and inclusive.

Our Mission

Typing Speed Test helps you measure and improve typing speed, consistency, and accuracy with real-time feedback and long-form practice. We prioritize accessibility (WCAG AA), performance, and a modern experience that’s enjoyable to use.

Our Approach

  • Accessibility-first: Keyboard navigation, ARIA live regions, and clear focus states.
  • Performance: Lightweight frontend and simple, secure backend.
  • Global: Multiple languages including RTL (Urdu), and layout hints.
