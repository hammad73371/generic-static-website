Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what we collect and why.

Policy

Data we collect

  • Anonymous test telemetry: WPM, accuracy, duration, language, timestamp.

How we use data

  • To display leaderboards and aggregate analytics.
  • To improve product features and performance.

Retention

Leaderboard and test entries may be retained for service quality and fraud prevention. You may request removal by contacting us.

Cookies

We may store local data (e.g., recent test averages) in your browser’s localStorage. We may use analytics cookies if configured.

Third parties

If enabled, analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics or Plausible) may collect usage data subject to their policies.

Contact

For privacy requests, email privacy@typingspeedtest.co.
